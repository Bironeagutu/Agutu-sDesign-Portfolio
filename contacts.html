How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. Residential projects typically take 3-6 months, while commercial projects can take 6-12 months or more.

Do you provide free consultations?

Yes! We offer free initial consultations to discuss your project requirements, budget, and timeline. This helps us provide accurate estimates and recommendations.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Nairobi and surrounding areas, but we also take on projects throughout Kenya. Contact us to discuss your specific location.

Yes, we assist with all necessary permits and approvals required for your project. Our team is experienced in navigating local regulations and requirements.
